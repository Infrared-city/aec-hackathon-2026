Analysis summary
The courtyard shows good thermal comfort in spring and autumn (UTCI 14–22°C). Wind from the south-west is the primary limiting factor, reaching Lawson C–D on the harbour edge. Direct sun hours are favourable at 4.1 h/day — sufficient for year-round use by elderly residents in the north-east quadrant.
Recommendations
01 Add a 3m windbreak screen along the south-west edge to reduce wind speed by ~40% and bring the harbour-facing zone to Lawson B.
02 Plant deciduous trees on the east side to provide summer shading without blocking low-angle winter sun — raising comfort to ~8.5.
03 Relocate seating clusters to the north-east quadrant where comfort scores are consistently highest (8.4/10) across all seasons.
